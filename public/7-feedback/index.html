Sharing and Feedback

Overall Evaluation

1. Working Environment The working environment at FCJ is exceptionally friendly, open, and professional. Members are always ready to support and guide me whenever I encounter difficulties, even beyond official working hours. The workspace is neat, comfortable, and well-organized, creating optimal conditions for interns to focus on research and development.

Minor Suggestion: The company might consider organizing occasional quick networking sessions or team-bonding activities to strengthen connections among members.

2. Mentor / Team Admin Support My mentor provided extremely dedicated guidance, explained core concepts with clear logic, and consistently encouraged critical thinking through every question. In particular, I deeply appreciate my mentor’s coaching methodology: empowering interns with autonomy to research, experiment, and solve problems independently rather than simply providing ready-made answers. The Team Admin team was also exceptionally thoughtful in supporting administrative procedures, documentation, and operational workflows.

3. Relevance to Academic Field The assigned tasks closely aligned with the foundational knowledge I acquired at university while expanding into specialized, high-impact practical engineering areas. As a result, I was able to both solidify my theoretical knowledge and quickly adapt to real-world software/infrastructure deployment standards.

4. Learning Opportunities & Skill Development The internship delivered immense value in both hard and soft skills. I mastered project management tools, enhanced my teamwork capabilities, and perfected professional communication skills within a corporate environment. The practical insights and experiences shared by my mentor played a pivotal role in clarifying my personal career path.

5. Culture & Team Spirit The company culture is highly positive: mutual respect, strong work ethic, and a lively, collaborative atmosphere. During tight project deadlines, the team spirit shone through as everyone actively supported one another regardless of position. This made me feel like an integral part of the team, despite being an intern.

6. Internship Policies & Benefits The company provides a reasonable internship allowance and offers flexible time management whenever academic schedules at university require it. Additionally, having the opportunity to participate in high-quality internal training sessions was a massive advantage for me.

ADDITIONAL QUESTIONS

  • What satisfied you the most during your internship?

It was the opportunity to directly participate in designing and deploying a real-world project (AI Riddle Generator) end-to-end from initiation to completion on AWS, under the direct guidance of Mr. Nguyen Gia Hung. I gained hands-on engineering experience with core AWS services such as AWS Lambda, API Gateway, DynamoDB, Amazon Cognito, and Amazon S3, along with cutting-edge AI technologies like Amazon Bedrock (Claude 3.5 Sonnet). Hands-on configuration of edge security systems (AWS WAF, Amazon CloudFront) and storage infrastructure significantly boosted my confidence in my technical capabilities. Furthermore, the respectful and open culture, combined with enthusiastic support from my teammates (Dang Quoc Tuan, Nguyen Duy Phuong, Ho Xuan Nguyen), created an immensely fulfilling internship experience.

  • What areas do you think the company could improve for future intern cohorts?

Because the AWS service ecosystem is vast and academic lab exercises sometimes differ from real-world enterprise deployments, I hope the company can provide standardized internal Reference Architectures right from the first week. This would help new interns shorten their onboarding curve and access systems more effectively.

  • If recommending to friends, would you advise them to intern here? Why?

Definitely Yes. This is an ideal internship environment for anyone aspiring to pursue a career as a Cloud Engineer or DevOps Engineer. Instead of being assigned trivial administrative tasks, AWS Vietnam interns are empowered to build real projects, learn systematically according to AWS Academy standards, cultivate problem-solving autonomy, and directly participate in high-value tech events to expand their mindset.

PROPOSALS & ASPIRATIONS

Proposal for Experience Improvement: I propose organizing short, periodic weekly cross-team project review sessions (Cross-team Reviews) among different intern groups so that everyone can cross-learn architecture and technology solutions from one another.

Future Aspirations: I strongly desire to continue my journey with AWS Vietnam as a full-time engineer or a collaborator supporting upcoming Cloud/AI projects.

Other Feedback: I would like to express my most sincere gratitude to Amazon Web Services Vietnam, especially Mr. Nguyen Gia Hung and all the office colleagues, for always patiently guiding me and providing the best conditions regarding infrastructure, AWS accounts, and an ideal workspace to complete my internship successfully.
